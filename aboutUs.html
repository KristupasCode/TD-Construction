About Us

About TG Decorators

TG Decorators is a reliable painting and decorating firm in London, with over 20 years of experience providing high-quality finishes to customers throughout the city. Our friendly and experienced team can breathe new life into homes and offices, leaving them clean and tidy. We have a wealth of knowledge on different painting techniques and products, and we are skilled in both interior and exterior jobs. Our expertise extends to commercial properties, and we always work punctually and to a high standard.

We offer domestic and commercial customers a broad range of painting and decorating services including:

  • Interior Decorating
  • Exterior Decorating
  • Painting
  • Wallpapering
  • Plastering
  • Coving
  • Quality Wall Coverings
  • Spraying
  • Tiling
  • Vinyl
  • Specialist Finishes
  • Redecoration

If you would like to discuss your specific requirements, please feel free to contact us with any questions that you may have or to arrange a no-obligation appointment. We look forward to hearing from you.
